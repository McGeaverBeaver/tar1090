Flight patterns — flag interesting flights from existing data
Scans the flights you already have and tags ones that match an interesting flying pattern, from their trail geometry: ✈ air show (known air-show type, or aerobatic maneuvering — confined, steep, repeated reversals), ◎ surveillance / orbit (sustained circling over a point — police / news / ISR / patrol), and ▦ aerial survey (parallel lawnmower grid — mapping / photography). Ordinary circuits and flight-school “up and down” are ignored. Results power the Patterns picker on the History page. Re-run any time after more history is logged or imported. Reads each flight's trail, so a large history can take a while.
